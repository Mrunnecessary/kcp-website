Our Story 🍫

We took a pause — life happened. But the love for handcrafted chocolate never faded. After two years, Kolkata Cocoa Parlor is back, bringing the same warmth and craft that made every box special.

Every chocolate you receive is made entirely by hand, from tempering to molding to packaging. No factory. No shortcuts. Just real chocolate made with real care.

Est. in Kolkata 🇮🇳
Our Promise

How It Works

From cocoa to your doorstep

No automation, no machines — every step done by hand.

01
Chat First

WhatsApp us to finalise flavours, quantity and price before placing the order.

02
Place Order

Fill the order form, agree to T&C, then pay via UPI shown on the confirmation screen.

03
We Craft

Your chocolates are made from scratch — hand-tempered, hand-molded. Minimum 10 days.

04
